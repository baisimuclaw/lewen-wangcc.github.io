Useful lab notes do not need to be long, but they should be easy to search and easy to trust.

## A lightweight checklist

- Write the purpose of the experiment in one sentence.
- Record the version of data, code, or instrument settings.
- Save the most important plot or observation.
- End with one next step.

This small structure helps me resume work quickly after switching between projects.
