A research workflow becomes much easier to sustain when notes, scripts, and outcomes stay connected.

## 1. Keep experiment notes close to code

I try to record the goal of each experiment, the exact inputs, and the main observation on the same day. This makes it easier to revisit unfinished ideas later.

## 2. Prefer small, repeatable scripts

Even for quick checks, lightweight scripts are easier to trust than manual operations. They also reduce the cost of rerunning an analysis after a small change.

## 3. Write short summaries after each iteration

A short written summary often saves more time than it costs. It helps me remember what worked, what failed, and which direction is worth continuing.
